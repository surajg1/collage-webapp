Pune District Education Association :

Pune District Education Association (PDEA) is well known educational society founded by the dedicated team of social reformers lead by Late Shri. Baburaoji Gholap and has a standing of over 77 years in the field of education. PDEA has received the prestigious, “Best Educational Institute Award” in the year 2000 by the Government of Maharashtra for the remarkable achievements in the field of education and social work. PDEA imparts education to rural and urban masses through Preprimary Schools (12), Primary Schools (13) Secondary Schools (55), Subsidiary School (06), Higher Secondary Schools (29), Senior Colleges (08), Ayurved College and Research Center (01), Pharmacy Colleges (02), Management Institutes (02), Polytechnic Institute (01), Engineering College (01), Law College (01), Architecture (01) and D.Ed. College (01). In the year 2006, inspired visionary and dynamic leader, Honorable Shri. Ajit Pawar took the charge as President of PDEA. Under his able guidance, all branches of PDEA’s are now taking a global speed to reach the destination.

Baburaoji Gholap College :

Baburaoji Gholap College runs 16 undergraduate courses and 11 postgraduate courses under the faculties of Arts, Commerce, Science, Computer Science and Computer Application. In addition to conventional courses, there are 5 B.Voc. Degree courses in Fashion Technology, Software Development, Beauty Therapy and Aesthetics, Retail Management and Travel and Tourism Management. College also offers ‘Diploma in Rubber Chemistry and Processing’ under UGC Community College Scheme. There are four Ph.D. Research centers in Economics, Physics, Chemistry and Environmental Science wherein 9 have been awarded with Ph.D. since 2012 and 106 students are currently pursuing their doctoral degree. An excellent ‘Sophisticated Instrumental Analysis Facility Centre (SIAFC)’ Laboratory for research activities is set up. The college received general development assistance from UGC, New Delhi and also funded with DBT-Star college scheme and DST-FIST scheme. Recently, college is selected for Unnant Bharat Abhiyan Scheme of UGC, New Delhi.

The college is recipient of the ‘Best College Award’ by Savitribai Phule Pune University in 2010; and first prize for “Jagar Janivancha” activity at University level by Government of Maharashtra in 2013 and 2014.
