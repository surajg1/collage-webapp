Pune District Education Association (PDEA) is well known educational society founded by the dedicated team of social reformers lead by Late Shri. Baburaoji Gholap and has a standing of over 77 years in the field of education. PDEA has received the prestigious, “Best Educational Institute Award” in the year 2000 by the Government of Maharashtra for the remarkable achievements in the field of education and social work. PDEA imparts education to rural and urban masses through Preprimary Schools (12), Primary Schools (13) Secondary Schools (55), Subsidiary School (06), Higher Secondary Schools (29), Senior Colleges (08), Ayurved College and Research Center (01), Pharmacy Colleges (02), Management Institutes (02), Polytechnic Institute (01), Engineering College (01), Law College (01), Architecture (01) and D.Ed. College (01). In the year 2006, inspired visionary and dynamic leader, Honorable Shri. Ajit Pawar took the charge as President of PDEA. Under his able guidance, all branches of PDEA’s are now taking a global speed to reach the destination.
